The Author

Bran McMillin is the creator of Puzzdrop, a puzzle magazine featuring original logic puzzles like Itachi and Hairetsu, alongside comics that blend humor and brain-twisting creativity.

Bran's work combines game design, art, and storytelling into playful, inventive experiences. He previously edited Panopticon: Puzzles & Games and Puzzle Pile, and has designed thousands of unique puzzles over the years. His projects explore the space between logic and imagination, where thinking hard can still make you laugh.
